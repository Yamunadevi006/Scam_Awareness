offline Scams

An offline scam is a type of fraud that happens without using the internet. Scammers often approach people face-to-face, over the phone, or through physical mail. They trick victims into giving money, personal information, or valuables. These scams can cause serious financial loss and identity theft if precautions are not taken.

Be Aware of This Scam

  • Do not give money or personal information to strangers at your door or over the phone.
  • Verify the identity of anyone claiming to be an official or service provider.
  • Avoid paying upfront for lottery winnings, job offers, or investment schemes.
  • Never share bank, ATM, or personal details with people in person.
  • Be cautious of strangers offering help or asking for donations.
  • Check all documents, receipts, or agreements carefully before signing.
  • Report suspicious visits, calls, or letters to local authorities immediately.
  • Educate family and friends about common offline scams to prevent victimization.

Phone&Message Scams

Phone and message scams use calls, texts, or messaging apps to trick people. Scammers often pretend to be banks, government officials, or delivery services. They may ask for personal information, OTPs, or payments. Victims can unknowingly share sensitive data or send money. These scams can lead to serious financial loss or identity theft.

Be Aware of This Scam

  • Never share your personal or financial information via calls or messages.
  • Always verify suspicious alerts through official channels.
  • Check phone numbers or email addresses carefully before responding.
  • Avoid clicking on links from unknown or unexpected messages.
  • Do not download attachments from suspicious messages.
  • Be cautious of messages claiming urgent action or prizes.
  • Report any suspicious calls or messages to your bank, mobile carrier, or authorities.

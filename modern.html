Modern Scams

Specialized or modern scams are advanced types of fraud that use new technologies, sophisticated tactics, or targeted methods to deceive victims. These scams often exploit social media, apps, online payments, cryptocurrencies, or personalized information to trick people. Scammers may impersonate companies, celebrities, or trusted individuals to gain trust and steal money or data. These scams are harder to detect and can cause significant financial and personal loss.

Be Aware of This Scam

  • Be cautious of suspicious links, apps, or messages on social media and messaging platforms.
  • Do not share passwords, OTPs, or personal information online, even with people claiming to be trusted sources.
  • Verify emails, websites, and online offers before making payments or sharing details.
  • Be careful of fake cryptocurrency, investment, or NFT schemes promising high returns.
  • Enable two-factor authentication on all accounts to add extra security.
  • Keep your devices updated with antivirus and security software.
  • Do not download unknown apps or software from unverified sources.
  • Report suspected online or modern scams to the cybercrime portal or relevant authorities immediately.

Be Aware of This Scam

  • Identity scams occur when fraudsters steal personal information like Aadhaar, PAN, or passwords.
  • Scammers use stolen identities to open bank accounts or take loans.
  • Never share personal details, OTPs, or verification codes with unknown people.
  • Avoid clicking suspicious links that ask for identity or login information.
  • Use strong, unique passwords for different accounts.
  • Enable two-factor authentication for emails and banking apps.
  • Regularly monitor bank statements and credit reports for unusual activity.
  • Report identity scams immediately to authorities or the cybercrime portal.

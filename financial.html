Financial Scams

A financial scam is a fraudulent act where scammers trick people into giving money or sensitive financial information. They often pretend to be banks, companies, or trusted individuals to gain trust. These scams can cause serious financial loss and identity theft.

Be Aware of This Scam

  • Never share your OTP, PIN, CVV, or bank details with anyone.
  • Do not click on unknown or suspicious links sent via SMS, email, or WhatsApp.
  • Verify bank calls and messages by contacting official customer care numbers.
  • Avoid loan or investment offers that promise quick money or high returns.
  • Check website URLs carefully before entering any financial information.
  • Do not make advance payments for loans, prizes, or job offers.
  • Enable SMS and email alerts for all bank transactions.
  • Report suspected financial scams immediately to your bank or cybercrime portal.
